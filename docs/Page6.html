2006 to 2007

Author: Terence Au

Yahoo's growth peaks and Google has exponential growth compared to 2005. Google grows almost 4x the year prior, on its way to becoming the top site visited in 2005. Other competitors such as Myspace, Amazon, Ebay, are all on the path to overtaking AOL at the third spot as well.

In 2007, Google overtakes the lead, breaking all numbers, while Yahoo peaks and falls back down. Even at the numbers the year before, google managed to almost double their visits in a year, compared to the year prior.
